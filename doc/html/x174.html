1.6. Known bugs and limitations

1.6.1. Version 1.0 / Patch 2.0.3

Reasonable testing has not shown any evident bugs for this particular release. But there is one issue that was not solved and its regarding locale. For some reason the module's specific locale scheme did not work and I simply did not have time to look further into the issue. You have to translate to your locale by adding the strings in the Tasks and Projects section of Translation Management and the patches to the locale files are not included in this version.

1.6.2. Future Upgrading Issues

Upgrading should work fine except that the data may not fit in the new database schema, because of the alters that Unitcost performs on some columns upon installation. It is recommended to verify this carefully, and backup you database well before attempting the upgrade. This is an issue that will have to wait for the next version until we can provide instructions on how to do this.
